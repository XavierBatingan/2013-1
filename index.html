The Pba (Pro Bowlers Association) is a major sport cooperation for the sport of professtional ten-pin bowling in the United States.

Each season, there are many different Pba tornaments where all of the pros compete to win that tournament. At the end of the season, the top 20 pros with the most wins on tour compete in the Tournament of Champions.
